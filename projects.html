    BOTSITIVITY

    Position: Writing Volunteer and Chief Literacy Officer

    January 2022 - Present

    BOTSITIVITY is a student-run non-profit organization whose mission is to support and raise awareness for mental health through the usage of machine learning by programming and launching a chatbot that can potentially prevent suicide and provide emotional assistance to its users. I began working with BOTSITIVITY in January 2022, became the first Volunteer of the Month in March 2022, and was promoted to become a board member as the Chief Literacy Officer in June 2022.

    Young Adult Service Program

    Position: Intern

    July 2022 - August 2022

    The Young Adult Service Program (YASP) connects Asian-American high school and college students with summer internships at various community organizations and the offices of local elected officials throughout New York City. In the summer of 2022, I had the opportunity to intern at Garden of Hope, a domestic abuse treatment center in Queens, NY.

    The World-Wide Teach-Ins on Climate Change

    Position: Intern

    January 2022 - April 2022

    The Worldwide Teach-Ins is an effort initiated by Bard Graduate Program in Sustainability to mobilize climate action and justice. During this internship, I designed social media posts to encourage climate action, networked on and managed an official account of the Worldwide Teach-In, and communicated and built a team with international interns.
